## 📂 Projects

### 🏦 [Amundi Client Investment Prediction](https://github.com/Lourenco10ucp/amundi-client-retention)
Built a three-stage ML pipeline in Python to optimise Amundi's weekly client outreach across 6,700+ leads under a 1,200-contact budget constraint. Combined classification (Random Forest, AUC 0.78) and regression (HistGradientBoosting, R² 0.97) models ranked by expected profit, generating €4,734 in realised holdout profit.

`Python` `scikit-learn` `XGBoost` `Predictive Analytics`

### 📊 [Huaxia Communications — Workforce Analytics](https://github.com/Lourenco10ucp/huaxia-workforce-analytics)
Regression analysis in R on 19 months of panel data from 135 employees to assess the impact of a natural WFH experiment on productivity, attrition and promotions. Found WFH reduced attrition by 16.3 percentage points and boosted productivity during a seasonal downturn.
